City Of London

London named the most picturesque city in the world Printful worked out what made a city picturesque by looking through Instagram hashtag data to discover the top 10 most picturesque cities in the world. When you think of London and picturesque, you might not think the two go together, but with the region's mix of historical and modern buildings, green spaces, flowing water and more, it makes sense London got the crown. The study discovered that London has more than 166 million Instagram hashtags, all capturing the beauty of the capital city.London's skyline is amongst the most picturesque, mixing a sea of green trees with glass skyscrapers and landmarks like the London Eye to the BT Tower. Closer to the ground, London offers views of historical bridges like Tower Bridge with its impressive design and the more modern and twisted Millenium Bridge.

Why London Is the Best City in the World

  • Famous landmarks like the London Eye, Big Ben, and Tower Bridge
  • Rich history and modern culture together
  • Beautiful parks and green spaces
  • Many job and business opportunities

Best Places to Visit in London

  1. The London Eye
  2. Big Ben and Houses of Parliament
  3. Beautiful parks and green spaces
  4. Excellent public transport system
